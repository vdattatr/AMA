Our Story

Three kids.
One big heart.

💛 Ashvika · Meher · Anisha

We are three young friends who believe that even the smallest acts of kindness can change the world. We started the AMA Lemonade Stand because cancer has touched people we love, and we wanted to do something about it.

Every cup of lemonade we pour, every donation we receive — 100% goes directly toward cancer research. No overhead. No middleman. Just kids squeezing lemons and squeezing out cancer.

AMA stands for Ashvika, Meher, and Anisha — and to us it means being brave, being kind, and turning every lemonade cup into a little ray of hope. 🍋💙
