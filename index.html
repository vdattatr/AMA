Our Story

Two kids.
One big heart.

💛 Ashvika & Meher

We are two best friends who believe that even the smallest acts of kindness can change the world. We started the AM Lemonade Stand because cancer has touched people we love, and we wanted to do something about it.

It all began one summer afternoon when we decided: instead of just feeling sad, we'd squeeze lemons — literally. We set up our stand, made fresh lemonade, and gave every single cent to kids fighting cancer at St. Jude's.

Every cup we pour, every donation we receive — 100% goes directly to St. Jude Children's Research Hospital. No overhead. No middleman. Just two friends squeezing lemons and squeezing out cancer. 🍋
